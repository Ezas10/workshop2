Watches

Watches appeared in the 16th century. During most of its history, the watch was a mechanical device, driven by clockwork, powered by winding a mainspring, and keeping time with an oscillating balance wheel. These are called mechanical watches.


Smart Watch

smartwatch, a small smartphonelike device worn on the wrist. Many smartwatches are connected to a smartphone that notifies the user of incoming calls, e-mail messages, and notifications from applications. Some smartwatches can even make telephone calls.


luxury watches

A luxury watch is defined not just by its price but by its exceptional craftsmanship, quality of materials, and brand heritage. These watches often come from high-end watch brands and are known for their durability, precision, and often, intricate designs.
